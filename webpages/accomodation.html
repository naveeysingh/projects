Our Bedrooms
Our every bedrooms are designed taking care about your comfort. Every room is facilitated with a 42" LED TV screen with mini home-theatre sound system to make you feel like you are in your own world. 24-hour wired and wireless internet access is provided in every room to communicate with your beloved and relatives anytime you want.

Price: Rs. 3000/room

Our Conference Hall
We also provide a conference hall in our hotel so that our guests can easily arrange business or any kind of meetings within the hotel. We have two kinds of conference hall: Large and Small Halls. We have 4 small conference halls and 2 large conference halls.
Small Conference Hall can take 10 members at a time, and Large Conference Hall can take 50 members at a time. All halls are designed with projector facility and sound system.

Price: Rs.2500 for a small meeting room
Price: Rs.4000 for a large meeting room

Our Party Hall
We have a party hall where you can organize any kind of party or event ceremony. We facilitate our guests with different continental dishes in catering service. The Party Hall can be decorated according to the guest's opinion. We have 2 party halls with all lighting equipment. Both halls are capable of holding 200 guests at a time.

Price: Rs.10,000 for the full day and night
